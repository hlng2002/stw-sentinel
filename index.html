🎯 Dual-Track Principle
  • Green (Worklet) — AudioWorklet scheduling interval, driven by OS audio subsystem, ~2.67ms/frame
  • Yellow (Main) — Main thread rAF frame interval, normally ~16.6ms (60fps)
  • Red dashed — STW threshold 10ms, exceeding means V8 GC Stop-The-World
🔬 Expected Behavior
  • 🌌 Supernova: Yellow spikes to hundreds of ms, green stays flat — proves thread isolation
  • 🔒 Main thread stall: Yellow vanishes for 200ms, green unaffected — lock-free proof
  • If green also spikes red, V8 triggered real STW — even Worklet was frozen
